Who we are

The inception of the Mourne Book Club took place in Rostrevor on a winter evening in McLoughlin's Bistro over an Irish coffee. We felt that the bibliophiles of Newry & Mourne were missing a place like this to relax and chat with other liked minded people. Since starting the club, we have grown, and continue to grow an amazing community. We hope you join and make many new friends!

Where we meet

We meet in the warm and cozy private lounge of McLoughlin's Bar & Bistro in Rostrevor. Situated at the foot of Kilbroney mountain and shore of Carlingford Lough, Rostrevor is any bibliophiles ideal destination for a book club. As C.S. Lewis put it in a letter to his brother, "That part of Rostrevor which overlooks Carlingford Lough is my idea of Narnia."
